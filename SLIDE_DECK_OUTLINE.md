# Sandchemy Pitch Deck Outline

## Slide 1: Title
- **Title**: Sandchemy
- **Subtitle**: The Professional Virtual Physics Laboratory
- **Visual**: A beautiful pixel-art simulation of the water cycle.

## Slide 2: The Problem
- Science education is too rigid and theoretical.
- Physical lab equipment is expensive, dangerous (chemicals/heat), and limited in scope.
- Students lose interest when they can't experiment freely.

## Slide 3: The Solution
- A fully interactive, offline-first cellular automaton engine running directly in the browser.
- Simulates real-world thermodynamics, chemical reactions, and biology.
- Safe, infinite, and zero-cost environment for limitless experimentation.

## Slide 4: Key Features (The Tech)
- **Logically Smart Physics:** Accurate heat transfer (Float32 precision), phase changes (melting/boiling points).
- **Ecosystem Simulation:** Autonomous creatures with food chains and environmental survival thresholds.
- **Nuclear Physics:** Safe simulation of radiation decay and thermal meltdowns.
- **Data Export:** Share entire worlds or custom elements via simple text strings (WhatsApp ready!).

## Slide 5: The Virtual Lab Use Case
- Teachers can load pre-built "Scenarios" (e.g., Acid-Base Reactions, Volcano Eruption).
- Students use the "Sensor Probe" tool to measure real-time temperatures and state changes.

## Slide 6: Business Model / Future
- Free core engine for students.
- Premium API or LMS integration for schools.
- Looking for educators to collaborate on curriculum design.
- Looking for scientists to refine the elemental reaction algorithms.

## Slide 7: Call to Action
- "Turn curiosity into discovery."
