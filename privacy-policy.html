Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Sandchemy is an educational virtual laboratory designed to be completely offline-first and privacy-respecting.

1. Data Collection

Sandchemy does not use a backend database. All simulation data, saved worlds, and custom elements are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage (`localStorage`). We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on remote servers.

2. Sharing Data

If you choose to export a custom element or a world save, the data is generated as a text string that you can manually share (e.g., via WhatsApp, email). This process is completely local to your device.

3. Third-Party Services

Sandchemy is hosted on GitHub Pages. Please refer to GitHub's privacy policy regarding standard web hosting analytics (IP addresses, user agents) that may be logged by their infrastructure.
