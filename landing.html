See it in action

A few of the reactions you can trigger yourself, the moment you open the sandbox — every one grounded in real physics and chemistry, not a canned animation.

🌋 Volcano Eruption

A prebuilt Scenario — a hollow stone mountain loaded with magma, ready to erupt the moment you step in.

🧪 Acid + Iron

Iron genuinely dissolves and releases real Hydrogen gas — Fe + 2HCl → FeCl₂ + H₂, simplified but real.

🧊 Ice + Lava

Heat radiates through open air — ice melts and lava cools measurably, even before they touch.

⚡ Lightning + Neon

~30,000°C lightning excites inert Neon into a genuine glow — and can fuse Sand into Glass in the same strike.

☢️ Radiation + Lead

Uranium decays into real Radiation — Lead genuinely blocks it, the same reason lead aprons exist.

🐟🐛 Ecosystem

Fish and Bugs with real survival thresholds — a light, food-chain-style touch of life among the chemistry.

Physics IS the crafting system

Most "elemental" games fake their combos — pour water on lava, get a scripted splash. Sandchemy simulates it: real heat, real density, real state changes. Pour water on lava and you don't just see an effect, you discover Obsidian — because that's what actually happens when lava quenches fast.
