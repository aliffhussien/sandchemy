Sandchemy

A professional virtual physics laboratory

Our Mission

To provide students, educators, and scientists with a powerful, accessible, and highly accurate cellular automaton engine to simulate physics, chemistry, biology, and thermodynamics directly in the browser.

Features

  • Advanced Thermodynamics: Real-time heat transfer, phase changes (melting/boiling), and thermal meltdowns.
  • Logically Smart Biology: Ecosystem simulation with creatures that follow food chains and environmental constraints.
  • Nuclear Physics: Radiation decay and atomic hazards.
  • Educational Scenarios: Pre-built lesson plans for classroom learning.

Collaborate with Us

Sandchemy is an open-source initiative. We are actively looking for educators to build custom lesson plans and scientists to refine our elemental algorithms.
